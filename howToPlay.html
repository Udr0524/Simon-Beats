How To Play!

Easy Mode

you press start, the computer takes its turn. Then you select the one the computer did. Now the computer goes again but this time 2 in a row. and to pass each level just mimic what it does.


Medium Mode

Where Medium and Easy differ is, that on Medium the computer only does its new one. you have to remember every one going up.


Hard Mode

where Hard Mode differs from Medium mode would be that the computer does 2 new squares per turn ontop of what you need to remember. so twice as hard as medium.


Free Play

You can just mess around and play with the drum kit either with mouse or keyboard 😁
